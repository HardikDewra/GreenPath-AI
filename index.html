Frequently Asked Questions

How Accurate are you Carbon Scores?

Our carbon scores are calculated using data from EPA, NOAA, and MIT research. We analyze the full product lifecycle including manufacturing, shipping, and disposal. Our algorithm is regularly audited by third-party environmental scientists and maintains 92% accuracy compared to detailed LCA studies. We update our database monthly with the latest environmental impact research.

Does this Work on Mobile?

Yes! GreenPath works perfectly on mobile browsers including Chrome, Safari, and Firefox on both iOS and Android. The mobile experience is optimized for on-the-go shopping with quick carbon score displays and easy product comparisons. Simply install our browser extension on your mobile device and shop sustainably anywhere.

How do you make money if it's FREE?

GreenPath is free for consumers forever. We earn revenue through our Premium subscription ($4.99/month) offering advanced features, and our Enterprise plan for businesses seeking certification. We also receive small affiliate commissions when you purchase through our sustainable product recommendations. We never sell your data or show ads - our mission is to make sustainable shopping accessible to everyone.

How Do I get my Business Certified?

Business certification starts with submitting your sustainability documentation through our Enterprise portal. Our team reviews your supply chain, manufacturing processes, and carbon footprint data. The certification process takes 2-4 weeks and includes on-site verification for larger operations. Certified businesses receive a GreenPath badge, priority listing in our recommendations, and access to our B2B sustainability network. Contact our sales team to start the certification process.

What Stores do you support?

GreenPath works on 50,000+ online stores including Amazon, Walmart, Target, eBay, Etsy, AliExpress, and thousands of independent retailers. We support all major e-commerce platforms including Shopify, WooCommerce, and Magento stores. If a store isn't supported yet, you can request it through the extension and we typically add new stores within 48 hours based on user demand.
